Zootopia——疯狂动物城

When i was a kid,

小时候，

I thought Zootopia was this perfect place.

我以为动物城是个很完美的地方。

Where everyone got along,

每只动物都和睦相处，

and anyone could be anything.

谁都能成就无限可能。

Turns out,

但现在发现，

real life is a little bit more complicated than a slogan on a bumper sticker.

现实比理想更复杂。

Real life is messy.

现实生活是糟糕的。

We all have limitations.

我们都有局限性。

We all make mistakes.

我们都会犯错误。

Which means,hey,glass half full,we all have a lot in common.

但是乐观的说，我们都很相通。

And the more we try to understand one another.

我们越是努力去了解别人，

The more exception each of us will be.

我们会变得越出色。

But we have to try.

但凡事都要尝试。

So no matter what type of animal you are.

不管你是什么类型的动物，

From the biggest elephant,to our first fox.

从巨大的大象，到第一只狐狸警官。

I implore you.

我恳请你们，

Try ...,try to make the world a better place.

努力试着让世界变得更美好。

Look inside yourself and recognize that change starts with you.

审视自己的内心，从改变自己开始

It starts with me.It starts with all of us.

从我做起，从我们做起。
